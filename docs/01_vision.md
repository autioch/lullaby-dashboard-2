# Product Vision

LaunchPad helps families answer:

> What do we still need to accomplish together before reaching our goal?

rather than:

> What tasks should I do?

The application transforms recurring routines into cooperative missions that provide:

- Shared visibility
- Coordination
- Progress awareness
- Reduced verbal reminders
- Positive reinforcement

The product should feel closer to a mission control center than a productivity/checklist application.

## Target Activities

- Morning routines
- Evening routines
- School preparation
- Travel preparation
- Weekly cleaning
- Guest preparation
- Event preparation

## Success Criteria

LaunchPad is successful when:

1. Family members look at the dashboard instead of asking what remains.
2. Routine completion time decreases over time.
3. Verbal reminders are reduced.
4. Completing routines feels rewarding.
5. Families voluntarily keep the dashboard visible.

## Product rules

- LaunchPad is cooperative, not competitive
- Themes never change game logic
- Dashboard is optimized for shared screens
- Remaining objectives have priority over completed objectives
- Mission terminology is preferred over task terminology
